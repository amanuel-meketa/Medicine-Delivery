About Us


BINI MDS is Boston’s trusted medicine delivery service, committed to providing fast, safe, and reliable access to essential medications. We understand that timely delivery of medicines is crucial for maintaining health and well-being, which is why our team is dedicated to ensuring that prescriptions and over-the-counter medicines reach our customers promptly and securely.



Our mission is to make healthcare more accessible, convenient, and dependable. Whether it’s routine prescriptions, urgent medical needs, or everyday wellness essentials, BINI MDS is here to support you and your loved ones with professional and caring service, 24/7.
